Projects

Selected projects

Projects focused on electronics hardware, sensors, embedded IoT, automotive-relevant monitoring, enclosures and field-ready products.

Environmental Electronics · Product Platform

CLEAR Environmental Monitoring Platform

Connected air quality and environmental monitoring platform for industrial and indoor environments. The system combines sensing hardware, embedded devices, telemetry, dashboards, alerts and analytics.

  • Worked with CO₂, particulate matter, VOC, NOx, temperature, humidity and noise monitoring.
  • Integrated ESP32-S3, SEN66, SPS30 and INMP441-based sensing workflows.
  • Supported enclosure design, electronics packaging, dashboard layout, testing and deployment readiness.
Industrial Electronics · Machine Monitoring

ORiV Current Sensor Utilisation System

ESP32-based current monitoring and machine utilisation device designed to track operating status, load behaviour, runtime and threshold events through a connected monitoring platform.

  • RMS current measurement and threshold-based logic.
  • Remote monitoring and request-driven telemetry through ThingsBoard.
  • Designed for machine utilisation tracking and legacy-machine digitisation.
Structural Monitoring · Battery IoT

StrucSense Concrete Monitoring Device

Wireless structural monitoring system for concrete applications using tilt, temperature, humidity and battery telemetry. Built around low-power operation and field-ready configuration.

  • MPU6050 pitch and roll measurement with baseline correction.
  • SHT31 temperature and humidity sensing.
  • Wi-Fi / LoRa configuration and battery-aware deployment logic.
Automotive-Relevant Testing · Motor Rig

Motor, Vibration and Flow Monitoring Rig

Developed monitoring workflows for a VFD-driven motor and pulley system, including vibration, current, flow, temperature and telemetry data capture for machine condition monitoring.

  • Worked with ADXL345, MPU6050 and industrial vibration sensors.
  • Captured fault-related data for anomaly detection and condition monitoring.
  • Linked industrial sensor outputs to embedded controller and dashboard workflows.
Mechanical Design · Electronics Enclosures

SolidWorks Enclosure Design

Designed multiple enclosures for CLEAR and smaller electronics projects, focusing on manufacturability, mounting, sensor exposure, serviceability and product identity.

  • Designed housings for embedded sensor hardware.
  • Supported DFM/DFA improvements and prototype refinement.
  • Considered cable routing, panel access, airflow and electronics packaging.
Rail Comfort · Distributed Sensing

Passenger Rail Comfort Monitoring Concept

Sensor-based monitoring concept for passenger rail comfort, combining environmental, air quality, vibration, light, location and noise data over long-range communication.

  • Sensor stack included noise, PM, air quality, CO₂, vibration, light, temperature, humidity and pressure.
  • Designed around distributed sensing and long-range communication.
  • Focused on comfort data collection and passenger environment insight.
