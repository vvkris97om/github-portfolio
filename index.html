Projects

Selected projects

Projects focused on deployable hardware, sensor intelligence, cloud telemetry and product engineering.

Environmental IoT · Product Platform

CLEAR Environmental Monitoring Platform

Cloud-connected air quality and environmental intelligence platform for industrial and indoor environments. The system combines sensing hardware, embedded firmware, MQTT telemetry, dashboards, alerts and analytics.

  • Worked with CO₂, particulate matter, VOC, NOx, temperature, humidity and noise monitoring.
  • Integrated ESP32-S3, SEN66, SPS30 and INMP441-based sensing workflows.
  • Supported enclosure design, dashboard development, remote monitoring and deployment readiness.
Industrial IoT · Machine Data

ORiV Current Sensor Utilisation System

ESP32-based machine utilisation and current monitoring device designed to track operating status, current thresholds, runtime behaviour, and remote telemetry through ThingsBoard.

  • RMS current measurement and threshold-based logic.
  • ThingsBoard RPC support for request-driven telemetry and remote settings.
  • Designed for industrial machine monitoring and operational visibility.
Structural Monitoring · Battery IoT

StrucSense Concrete Monitoring Device

Wireless structural monitoring system for concrete applications using tilt, temperature, humidity and battery telemetry. Built around low-power operation and field-ready configuration.

  • MPU6050 pitch and roll measurement with baseline correction.
  • SHT31 temperature and humidity sensing.
  • Wi-Fi / LoRa configuration and battery-aware deployment logic.
Condition Monitoring · Motor Rig

Motor, Vibration and Flow Monitoring Rig

Developed and tested monitoring workflows for a VFD-driven motor and pulley system, including vibration, current, flow, temperature and telemetry data capture for condition monitoring.

  • Worked with ADXL345, MPU6050 and industrial vibration sensors.
  • Collected fault-related data for anomaly detection and AI model training.
  • Integrated industrial sensor outputs into controller and cloud workflows.
Mechanical Design · Enclosures

SolidWorks Enclosure Design

Designed multiple product enclosures for CLEAR and smaller electronics projects, focusing on manufacturability, mounting, sensor exposure, serviceability and product identity.

  • Designed housings for embedded sensor hardware.
  • Supported DFM/DFA improvements and prototype refinement.
  • Considered cable routing, panel access, airflow and electronics packaging.
Rail Comfort · LoRa Sensing

Passenger Rail Comfort Monitoring Concept

Sensor-based monitoring concept for passenger rail comfort, combining environmental, air quality, vibration, light, location and noise data over LoRa-based communication.

  • Sensor stack included noise, PM, air quality, CO₂, vibration, light, temperature, humidity and pressure.
  • Designed around distributed sensing and long-range communication.
  • Focused on comfort data collection and passenger environment insight.
